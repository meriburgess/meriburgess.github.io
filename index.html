Meredith Burgess

Developer Performer

Bio

Meredith is an artist turned computer scientest, as well as circus extraordinaire. Her interests include the study of movement, particularly that which deals with human activity recognition and the use of machine learning algorithms. She dreams of combining such things with creative works as live performances and installations of all kinds.

Meredith is currently a student at the University of Colorado Boulder and works as an undergraduate researcher in the Interactive Robotics and Novel Technologies Lab on campus.
